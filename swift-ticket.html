Project Overview

What is Swift Ticket?

🎯
The Problem
Traditional ticket booking systems involve long queues and manual cash transactions. These processes are time-consuming, prone to errors in managing tickets and records, and make it difficult to monitor transactions efficiently.
💡
Our Solution
An NFC card linked to a digital wallet that enables instant contactless ticket booking. The system identifies the user, checks the wallet balance, deducts the fare automatically, and prints the ticket immediately — no cash, no queues.
🏆
Achievement
Developed and showcased at the NAIN Innovation Conclave held at IIT Bombay, Bengaluru on April 2, 2026, under the New Age Incubation Network 2.0 initiative of the Government of Karnataka. Awarded Certificate of Completion & Appreciation.

Workflow

How It Works

1
NFC Card Scan
The user taps their NFC card on the ESP32-based vending machine. The system instantly reads the card ID and identifies the registered user.
2
Wallet Balance Check
The system retrieves the user's digital wallet balance and displays it on screen, confirming whether sufficient funds are available for the selected ticket.
3
Ticket Selection
The user selects their desired ticket from the available options. The fare is shown before confirmation, ensuring full transparency.
4
Automatic Deduction
Upon confirmation, the ticket amount is automatically deducted from the user's digital wallet — no cash handling, no manual processing.
5
Instant Ticket Printing
The thermal printer integrated with the machine instantly prints the physical ticket, completing the entire booking process in seconds.

Capabilities

Scope of the Application

Android-Based System — Mobile app handles both admin-side operations (wallet recharge, user management) and user-side operations (balance check, booking history) seamlessly.
NFC-Based Identification — Each user is assigned a unique NFC card for contactless, secure, and fast identification at the vending machine kiosk.
Wallet-Based Automated Booking — Prepaid digital wallets eliminate the need for cash transactions, speeding up the entire ticketing process.
Admin-Controlled Wallet Recharge — Administrators can remotely recharge user wallets through the Android app, ensuring users always have sufficient balance.
Thermal Printer Integration — A hardware thermal printer connected to the ESP32 unit produces physical ticket receipts instantly after every successful booking.
